How to Play
Playing GUNDI

Uganda's word guessing party game

1
Teams & Timer — Set up your teams (2–6 players) and choose a timer: 30, 60, or 90 seconds per turn.
2
Draw a Card — Each card has a set of words. The active team's clue-giver sees all the words.
3
Give Clues — Describe each word using gestures, sounds or clues — no spelling it out! Teammates guess.
4
Tap to Score — Tap a word when your team guesses it correctly. Skipped words score nothing.
5
Pass & Score — When the timer ends or all words are guessed, the team earns 1 point per correct word.
6
Win — Play through all cards or set a score target. The team with the most points wins!
💡 Pro Tip
The bold/highlighted words on each card are worth double — they're the tricky ones!
